Current Visit Types

Visit Type Duration (mins) Description
1 month F/U 15 Regular follow-up check-up.
3 month F/U 15 Regular follow-up check-up.
6 month F/U 15 Regular follow-up check-up.
Adult Wellness 30 A comprehensive preventative health assessment for adults.
Blood Pressure Check 15 Quick appointment to monitor blood pressure.
Chronic Care Managment 15 Ongoing management of two or more long-term health conditions.
Surgery Clearance 15 Pre-operative assessment to ensure patient is fit for surgery.
Discuss Meds 15 Appointment to review and discuss patient medications.
DOT Physical 30 Physical examination required for commercial drivers (Department of Transportation).
Emergency Room F/U 30 Follow-up visit after a recent emergency room visit.
Established Patient 15 A standard visit for a patient already established with the practice.
Follow Up Visit 15 General follow-up appointment.
Hospital F/U 30 Follow-up visit after a hospital stay or discharge.
Injection only 15 A quick appointment for an injection or shot.
Lab Draw 15 Appointment for phlebotomy/drawing blood for lab tests.
Med Refill 15 A quick visit to authorize a prescription refill.
New Patient 30 First-time visit for a patient new to the practice.
Newborn 30 A well-child check or visit for a newborn patient.
Radiology 15 Appointment for a specific radiology service (e.g., X-ray).
Referral 15 Visit to discuss or coordinate a referral to a specialist.
Same Day Appointment 15 Urgent appointment scheduled for the same day.
Sports Physical 30 Physical exam required for participation in sports.
Telehealth 15 Virtual appointment via phone or video conference.
Ultrasound 15 Appointment for an ultrasound procedure.
Vaccination 15 Appointment for administration of a vaccine/immunization.
Walk in sick 15 Visit for a patient who walks in for acute illness care.
Workmans Comp 15 Visit related to a workplace injury claim (Worker's Compensation).
Well child exam 30 A comprehensive preventative health check for a child.
Well Women exam 30 A comprehensive preventative health check for women, including a physical and necessary screenings.
Sick Visit 15 An appointment for a patient experiencing acute illness symptoms.
Establish Care/New 30 A comprehensive initial visit for a patient new to the practice to establish a patient-physician relationship.
