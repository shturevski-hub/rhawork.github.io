Base Rate Visit Charge

One of the following charges **MUST** be applied to every Self-Pay Visit. Additional charges may apply depending upon the services provided and are in addition to the Base Rate Charge. See the list of Add-on Service Charges below.

"Self-Pay" Office Visit: Includes basic in-house tests, injections, x-rays, and procedures. $125
Behavioral Health Initial Visit: Scheduled visit with a Behavioral Health Team. A separate drug screen fee applies if needed. $150
Behavioral Health Follow-up Visit: For subsequent visits with a Behavioral Health Team. $90
Telehealth Visit: Scheduled virtual visit. Additional testing is not included. $85
EPSDT, Well Visits, or DOT Physicals: This fee applies to all Early and Periodic Screening, Diagnostic, and Treatment (EPSDT) visits, Well Visits, and Department of Transportation (DOT) physicals. $125
Simple Work Physical, Childcare, Sports, or Basic Student Physical: This applies to one-page physical forms. Additional testing fees may apply. $25
Forms Fees (e.g., FMLA paperwork) $25
